Recommendation: 02 · Navy Gold
The diamond mark is geometrically precise, the Archivo 200 wordmark is ultra-refined, and navy + gold is the most universally understood palette for premium, trustworthy, enterprise-grade products. It will age exceptionally well — no trend dependency. Closest comparable identities: Stripe, Linear, Notion in their most serious modes.

Best runner-up: C1 Aperture (21/25) — the photography metaphor is thematically perfect for an ad creative platform, and the coral accent reads energetic but controlled.
Terminal and Neon Pop score lowest on enterprise trust and timelessness — not because they are poorly designed, but because monospace/neon aesthetics carry a "side project" signal that works against a platform selling to marketing teams. Spark K reads consumer/lifestyle rather than B2B SaaS.
